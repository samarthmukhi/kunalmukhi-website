My Vision

"I don't measure success in policies sold — but in claims paid, promises kept, and relationships that outlast decades."

I am the third generation of my family to work in insurance, so I grew up knowing that a policy is a promise. Yet even then, I saw too many families discover — at the worst possible moment — that they were not truly protected. I founded Insurance Services (Group) India to change that.

My vision is simple: that every Indian family and business, whatever their means, has honest access to the right protection — explained in plain language and backed by someone who answers the phone when it matters most.

As I look ahead, I want to carry that standard across the country through a network of advisors who share it — bringing the same integrity to the next generation of Indian insurance.

About Kunal

An engineer who found his calling in protecting people.

The third generation of his family in insurance — carrying a legacy of protecting people, built one relationship at a time.

Insurance is in Kunal's blood. As the third generation of his family to make protecting people their life's work, he grew up understanding that a policy is a promise — and that a family's trust, once earned, is a responsibility carried across generations.

Kunal Mukhi began his own chapter in insurance in 2003 with Reliance General Insurance, learning marketing and risk management from first principles. In 2004 he founded Insurance Services (Group) India and has since spent more than twenty years building a pan-India network of agents and a book of profitable, dependable retail and commercial business.

As a Service Provider Franchisee for IFFCO-Tokio General Insurance and an authorised advisor across leading insurers, the firm places the right cover with the right underwriter — never the other way around.
